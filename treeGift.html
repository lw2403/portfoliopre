Tree Gift App

Product Design

Overview

Trella Tree Gift is an online tree gifting app designed for virtual tree buying, gifting, and planting experience. It targets people who are environmentally cautious and want to plant trees for social good together with their friends. Through efforts to conduct user interviews, gain expertise advice, and collect data from iTrees, the app includes features such as naming the tree, building a personal forest, checking tree diaries, and gifting to friends.

Timeline: 3 Months

Tools: Adobe XD

Context: Startup Project

Solution

Shopping

The home page has a tree shopping area with different filters. Users can view trees with special meanings such as friendship or anniversary. Each tree item provides information about the tree‚Äôs eco-benefits, values, and price.

Facts

Each tree‚Äôs detail page provides facts about the tree and the values associated with it. Some trees have special meanings while others have bigger environmental benefits. The app is also built for educational purposes.

Gifting

Trella tree gift offers users a choice to package the tree gift. There‚Äôs a tree gift card section where users can select a background and add a unique message to their beloved ones. Users may also use their own photos as the background.

Forest

The app tries its best to give a virtual forest experience. Users can view the trees they have collected as gifts from others and the user‚Äôs total contribution to the environment with 4 eco benefit values. The values will be a cumulative value from all the trees the user owns. In the future, the background will also be a video of the forest for people to see.

Tree Diary

Users can see updates from their trees which they also have the naming rights. They may also interact with the posts by clicking the like button.

Value of Purchase

Studio Feedback:Interviewees think there is not enough information on the user experience of the app once they make a purchase.

Solution:We added a short 30-second tutorial in the home page prior to purchase and illustrate to users the experience of post purchase includes both video of how the tree is growing in real life and the app experience. In the future, we will also include game features such as watering the tree.

Purpose of App

Studio Feedback:Interviewees were familiar with the company‚Äôs concept prior suggested they liked how tree facts and environmental are embedded in the app. Survey responses suggest there needs to be more explanation.
